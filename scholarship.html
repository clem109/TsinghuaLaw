Scholarship

LL.M. Program provides two kinds of scholarships The applicants may apply for Mingli Scholarship and Beijing Government Scholarship (BGS), The scholarships are categorized into full scholarships and partial scholarships. Full scholarships cover whole tution, accommodation fee and living fee (1700 yuan per month). Partial scholarships cover 10,000 yuan or 20,000 yuan.

The scholarships are preferentially awarded to the full-year applicants from developing countries study at Tsinghua University.

The deadline for scholarship application is Feb.28, 2017.
