FAQs


  • question_answer1. Who is eligible for admission?

    Our program aims at those who are either current law graduates or students, or practicing lawyers. An applicant must meet the following requirements:

    1) J.D., LL.B. or equivalent law degree;
    2)Enrollment in a J.D. program; or
    3)Qualification to practice law.

  • question_answer2. How long will the LL.M. program take?

    The duration of the program is 1 year (two semesters). If a student can only stay for one semester for the first time, he/she may come again for another semester within 2 years, in order to get the degree.

  • question_answer3. Does Tsinghua admit students for only one semester?

    The duration of the program is 1 year (two semesters). If a student can only stay for one semester for the first time, he/she may come again for another semester within 2 years, in order to get the degree.

  • question_answer4. Is an interview required?

    Interviews are not required, but the admissions committee may conduct telephone interviews when there is a such need.

  • question_answer5. What are the language requirements?

    This program will be conducted in English and all the materials will be in English as well. No Chinese language skill is required. Those who have Chinese do have an advantage in using more materials. We will also provide a Chinese language course for those who are interested while studying at Tsinghua. More intensive Chinese teaching could be arranged individually by the students at their own costs.

  • question_answer6. How many electives courses do I have to take?

    As the LL.M. Program requires a minimum of 24 credits of course studies, and generally each course consists of 2-3 credits, an LL.M. student may select 4 to 5 elective courses that interest him/her the most.

  • question_answer7. How will my performance in the LL.M program be evaluated?

    There is no comprehensive examination at the end of the LL.M. program. A score will be given to the each course based on examinations or papers. Most of the required courses have in-class-examinations and most of the elective courses have take-home-examinations. The examinations may take the forms of written essay or question s-and answers at the decision of the professors and teachers.

  • question_answer9. Is financial aid available?

    Since this program is to be offered for the first time, we currently do not have scholarship available. We are trying to make scholarships offered by some institutions and firms. Therefore, applicants are strongly encouraged to seek funding elsewhere, from a variety of public and private sources, and to contact their home governments and employers.

  • question_answer10. Is there any on-line payment system I can use to make the application payment?

    Yes, there is. When your application on line has been verified, you will receive the link for the payment by email. Then please pay on line.

  • question_answer11. Do all the students who attend the LL.M. Program need to take the physical examination?

    Yes. All the students who study the LL.M. Program are required to do the physical examination.

  • question_answer12. How to do the physical examination?

    Please do the physical examination adhering to the “Foreigner Physical Examination Form” and pay attention to the followings:

    a. Make sure that the doctor sign on EVERY page of the “Foreigner Physical Examination Form” ; and
    b. Make sure that the doctor stamps on your photo on the first page of the “Foreigner Physical Examination Form”; and
    c. Make sure to bring all the original physical examination results including the X-Ray together with your “Foreigner Physical Examination Form” and let your doctor sign on EVERY copy of the original physical examination result. and
    d. If your doctor can not give all the original physical examination results, you are required to do it again in China. So you are suggested to make sure that you can get all the original copies in order to avoid spending the extra expenses when you come to china.

  • question_answer14. Should all the students finish their study within one year?

    It depends. The students can finish their study in LL.M. Program within three years.
