Zijing International Student Apartments


The university offers comfortable dormitories with easy access to the university's classrooms, faculty offices and other facilities. They house students from all over China and around the world. Students who want to stay with Chinese families or rent apartments available in the market may do so by their own arrangement.

The Zijing Apartments provide three types of dormitory rooms, with a capacity of accommodating around 2000 international students each year.

The facilities and prices of the rooms are listed below:

Type Price Facilities
Single Room RMB 80 Yuan/Day Private bedroom and bathroom/toilet
AB Double Room RMB 80 Yuan/Day Private bedroom, shared bathroom/toilet
Double Room Two beds to a room, shared bathroom/toilet on each floor RMB 40 Yuan/Day

All rooms have the following: air-conditioner, telephone, TV, and internet access. The prices listed above may subject to change by the Zijing Property Management Center. The General Reception Desk of Zijing International Student Apartments is in Zijing Apartment Building No.19. It serves from 7.00 am to 23.00 pm. Service phone number: +86-10-5153 5500; Email: gaopei@tsinghua.edu.cn.
